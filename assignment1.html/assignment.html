About BookHive

Our story, our mission, our love for books

Our story

BookHive was born in 2024 from a simple idea — every Indian reader deserves easy access to the best books from around the world, at fair prices, delivered to their doorstep.
Founded in Bengaluru by a small team of book enthusiasts, we've grown into India's go-to destination for fiction, self-help, biographies, business books, and more.
We believe books have the power to change lives, spark new ideas, and connect people across cultures.
